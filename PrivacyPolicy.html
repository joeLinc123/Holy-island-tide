PRIVACY POLICY

We take your privacy very seriously, and we will only use data relevant to our processes.

By using this service, you are agreeing to this policy, which outlines:

  • What data we use
  • What we use that data for
  • How we manage your details

The data we use:

We capture the following data about you:

  • Your Twitter Handle, should you have one.
  • Your email
  • Your postcode, this may be your home postcode, or the location which you're leaving from

We will never ask you your name, instead you will have a username. We reccomend this to be different from your actual name.

What we use this data for

  • Your Twitter handle is used for communication purposes, so we can send you a warning notification
  • Your Email address is required to send warning notification, so you can safely depart the island
  • A source location is required to calculate the time of your journey to Holy island

How we manage your data

We will never keep your data for longer than is necessary. Your user details will be kept, in case you wish to use our service again. Journey data will be archived and then deleted after 30 days.
